When Does the Organization Need a New Management Layer?
Based on research: effective span of control for knowledge workers is 5-7 direct reports. Above 8 degrades decision quality. Above 10 creates operational bottlenecks.
25
Current State
5 managers, avg span of 4.0. Healthy. Room to absorb 5-10 new hires before strain appears.
40
Trigger Point
At 40 employees with 5 managers, average span reaches 7.0. Amber zone. This is when you need to either hire 2-3 new managers or promote from within.
60
Breaking Point
At 60 employees with 5 managers, average span is 11.0. Every manager is a bottleneck. You needed the new layer 20 hires ago. The cost of waiting is higher than the cost of acting.
